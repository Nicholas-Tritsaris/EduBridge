Frequently Asked Questions

Is EduBridge free?

Yes, EduBridge is free to use for educators and students. We also offer premium features for organizations.

Can I use EduBridge offline?

Currently, EduBridge requires an internet connection. We are working on offline capabilities.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your data.

Can I import data from other platforms?

Yes, we support importing data from common LMS platforms. Contact support for assistance.
